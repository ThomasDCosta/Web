About Us

Welcome to our web application dedicated to monitoring and displaying real-time air quality data. Our goal is to provide users with accurate and accessible information about various atmospheric pollutants, promoting a better understanding and awareness of environmental issues.

Our platform allows you to track several crucial parameters in real time, including:

  • Temperature: Monitor temperature variations in your area to better understand the impact of climatic conditions on air quality.
  • Humidity: Discover the ambient humidity levels, an important factor that can influence air quality and health.
  • PM2.5 and PM10: These fine particles are major pollutants that can have harmful effects on human health. Track their concentration to make informed decisions about outdoor activities.
  • NOx (Nitrogen Oxides): Monitor NOx levels, pollutants often emitted by vehicles and industrial facilities.
  • NH3 (Ammonia): Evaluate ammonia concentrations, another atmospheric pollutant with significant environmental impacts.
  • CO2 (Carbon Dioxide): Track CO2 levels, a major greenhouse gas, to understand its influence on climate change.
  • SO2 (Sulfur Dioxide): Stay informed about SO2 concentrations, a pollutant associated with fossil fuel combustion.
  • VOC Levels (Volatile Organic Compounds): VOCs can have harmful effects on health and indoor air quality. Our application helps you monitor them.

With a user-friendly interface, our site offers various options to explore this data, such as interactive charts, personalized reports, and pollution level alerts. Whether you're a health-conscious citizen or an environmental professional, our application is designed to meet your air quality monitoring needs.

We believe that a better understanding of the air we breathe can lead to healthier choices and a more sustainable future. Join us in our mission to raise awareness and educate about air quality!
